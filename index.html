This Website is to assist in getting started with DnD.

DnD is a table-top roleplaying adventure game that has taken the world by storm. With its insanely in depth mechanics and systems and Tolkien-esque lore, it is by far the closest we can get to entering the worlds of elves and dragons.

In this game you play as one of a number of adventurers. You along with the other players with you will form a party that will travel the land while adventuring. Throughout the game the world and whats happening in it will be relayed to you by the Dungeon Master(DM). The Dm is the person who writes the story for the campaign and even creates the world you play in.

In the world of DnD you can be whatever you want. You can become a tyranicle ruler, an evil villain, a hero, or even a god. The limits of the DnD world are only limited by imagination. There are technically no rules as to what you can do in the game. There are certain restrictions on your characters physical capabilities, but if you wanted to completely stop adventuring and set up a pet shop you could, thats just how expansive this game is.
