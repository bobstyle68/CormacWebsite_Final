This is a typical DnD map. Most maps in DnD consist of a main continent and a couple of smaller continents around that main one. Most campaigns start on the main continent because it gives the players access to all of the other continents for their quests. This map follows that general layout. The smaller continents tend to be the home of the less human like races of DnD, like Dragonborn for example. Dragonborn are descended from dragons so they tend to live in incredibly warm environments, sometimes going as far as living on a volcano.
