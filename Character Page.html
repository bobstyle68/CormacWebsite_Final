This is a table displaying information on all of the base classes on the game. This table can be used as reference when creating a DnD character.

Class: DC(Dice Roll): Brief Description:
Barbarian Roll with Strength Barbarians specialise in using brute force, dont use any armor and have high damage and health.
Bard Roll with Charisma Typically friendly and good with their words, cast spells with music and have low health. Usually support characters, but can be used as a frontline fighter if necessary.
Cleric Roll with Charisma Clerics are a typically healers, but if played right can deal out massive damage on the first level, making them a very popular choice for veteran players.
Druid Roll with Wisdom Druids are at one with nature, they can use various spells and rituals to both summon and transform into animals.
Fighter Roll with Dexterity Fighters typically use most weapons and are a balance between attack and defence, this makes them a very versatile and capable of supporting many types of builds.
Monk Rolls with Wisdom Monks are martial artists and study the way of the fist. They typically attack fast and accurately, often managing to land many successful blows in a single turn.
Paladin Rolls with Charisma Paladins believe in enforcing justice, even if it sometimes means breaking the law. Paladins take an oath, and if they break that oath there could be serious repercussions. Paladins are typically high defence and usually take alot of attacks so other players can get attacks on the enemy.
Ranger Rolls with Dexterity Rangers are usually hunters and trackers. They are masters of survival and are excellent at archery.
Rogue Rolls with Dexterity Rogues are usually thieves or assassins. They specialise in attacking from the shadows to deal high damage and apply status abnormalities that will slow down the enemy.
Sorcerer Rolls with Charisma Sorcerers use there will to change the world around them, and this change takes the form of magic. Sorcerers dont specialise in a single magic school, instead using magic of all sorts to gain the advantage on the battlefield.
Warlock Rolls with Charisma Warlocks summon spirits to fight for them, because of this many people believe they are evil. They either sweet talk the spirit into entering a contract with them, or torment them and force them to fight for them.
Wizard Rolls with intelligence Wizards above all else are scholars, this is why they learn their magic through rigorous study. Wizards value knowledge above all else, and it is knowledge that usually persuades them to go on an adventure.
